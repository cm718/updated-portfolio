About Me

Hello, my name is Chris. I currently work for Apple and I decided to start learning to code at the recommendation of a friend. He had gone through a coding boot camp at Northwestern University and suggested I explore it as an option.

I started learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript on my own alrady. I really enjoyed the challenge and sense of success when things worked. As the months went by I found myself really loving the learning process. My wife and I began to discuss the possibility of a career change. I was learning more about web development as well as the wonderful job market that exists for developers.

I decided that when the next boot camp opened up I would apply and here I am today. I look forward to refining what I already know as well as building on my current skills, specifically with Javascript and back end development.
